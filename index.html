What is MIHOYO?


MiHoYo, founded in 2011, is a technology company deeply cultivating animation culture. Over the years, MiHoYo has adhered to the independent innovation of technology and adhered to the road of original high-quality products, and built a whole industry chain covering comics, animation, games, music, novels and animation around original IP.Although its games are few, but each is a boutique. In particular, it is widely praised for its artistic level. His work Genshin has a considerable reputation all over the world.
